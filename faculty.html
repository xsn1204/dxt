Biography

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Chemistry at the University of Pennsylvania, working under the supervision of Prof. Macus Kozlowski. My research lies at the interface of High-Throughput Experimentation (HTE) and Oxidative Coupling Catalysis.

I received my B.S. in Chemistry from Boston University and my Ph.D. from Scripps Research, where I focused on the total synthesis of complex natural products. Currently, I am developing machine-learning-guided workflows to optimize transition-metal-catalyzed reactions.
