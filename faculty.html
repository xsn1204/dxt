Biography

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Perelman School of Medicine, University of Pennsylvania. My research focuses on regenerative medicine, specifically developing bioactive scaffolds for accelerated skin wound healing.

I received my B.S. in Biomedical Sciences from Boston University and earned my Ph.D. from Johns Hopkins School of Medicine, where my doctoral work centered on stem cell niche modulation and biomaterial interface design.
